Characters

Ross Geller (David Schwimmer) - A paleontologist with a complicated romantic life, particularly his on-off relationship with Rachel. Monica's older brother. Rachel Green (Jennifer Aniston) - Initially a waitress, Rachel later pursues a career in fashion. Ross's love interest and Monica's childhood friend. Monica Geller (Courteney Cox) - Ross's meticulous younger sister, a chef, and a nurturing figure within the group. Chandler Bing (Matthew Perry) - Known for his sarcastic humor, Chandler works in IT and advertising. He has a significant relationship with Monica. Joey Tribbiani (Matt LeBlanc) - An aspiring actor with a love for food and a carefree personality. Phoebe Buffay (Lisa Kudrow) - A quirky and free-spirited musician with an unusual past.
